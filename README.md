## 🎙️ About the Show

**Practical AI** is a podcast where technology professionals, business people, students, enthusiasts, and expert guests engage in lively discussions about Artificial Intelligence and related topics.

> *"If you want to keep up with the latest advances in AI, while keeping one foot in the real world, then this is the show for you!"*

The focus is on **productive implementations** and **real-world scenarios** that are accessible to everyone — not just researchers and engineers.
